## About Bella

I am an MLIS student. This GitHub page is dedicated to my Aunt's rescue dog, Bella.  Bella and her sister were found in a box in an alley in Maryland.  They were brought to a veterinary hospital, which happens to be run by my cousin Amber.  She evaluated the two puppies and decided to adopt one.  The other, she offered to my Aunt, her grandmother, as a good companion. 

Bella is a handful, but so loving.  It was originally thought that she would only grow to be about 50 lbs.  That is not the case, as she is at least 100 lbs of pure muscle.  Bella is only three years old right now.  She loves annoying my Aunt's other dog, Neville, another rescue dog, who is 14 years old.  You may see him in the background of the pictures I uploaded, but mostly he likes to hide from Bella to find a place to take a peaceful nap.  

Even though Bella is loud, weighs a ton when she sits on you (she thinks she's a lap dog), and can act like a bull in a china shop, I wouldn't trade her for the world.
